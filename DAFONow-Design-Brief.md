# DAFONow — Master Design Brief

## PART 1 — PRODUCT CONTEXT

**Product Name:** DAFONow  
**Organization:** Cascade Dafo  
**Product Type:** Customer-facing clinical ordering platform  

**Product Purpose:**  
Enable orthotists to:
- Order DAFOs faster
- Reduce errors
- Track orders
- Save drafts
- Resume work
- Manage patients
- Improve clarity
- Improve confidence

---

## PART 2 — DON'S VISION & EMOTIONAL CONTEXT

This product is not just software. This is Don's dream.

**Don wants:**
- Simpler ordering
- Less friction
- Better customer experience
- Improve patient outcomes
- Improved accuracy
- Reduced confusion
- Modernize Cascade experience
- Build trust with customers
- Make Cascade easy to work with

**Don's emotional drivers:**
- Frustration with complex ordering
- Pride in craftsmanship
- Customer-first mindset
- Desire to modernize
- Reduce orthotist stress
- Improve experience without losing precision

**Design Implication — The product must feel:**
- Calm
- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Intelligent
- Not flashy
- Not consumer-like

The experience should blow away Don by demonstrating deep understanding of:
- Cascade
- Orthotists
- Industry
- Workflow complexity

---

## PART 3 — INDUSTRY CONTEXT

**Industry:** Orthotics / DAFO industry

**Users:**
- Orthotists
- Clinicians
- Clinic admins

**User Environment:**
- Busy clinics
- Interruptions
- Multi-tasking
- High pressure
- Accuracy critical
- High cognitive load
- Low tolerance for complexity

**User priorities:**
- Accuracy
- Confidence
- Clarity
- Speed

**Not:**
- Fancy UI
- Marketing aesthetics
- Complexity

---

## PART 4 — USER PERSONAS

### Persona 1 — Orthotist (Primary)
**Goals:** Submit correct order, Save time, Resume work, Reduce back and forth  
**Pain points:** Complex forms, Interrupted workflows, Hard to find orders  
**Needs:** Drafts, Search, Tracking, Resume

### Persona 2 — Clinic Admin
**Goals:** Track orders, Manage patients, Follow up  
**Needs:** Global search, Tracking visibility

### Persona 3 — Power User
**Goals:** Speed, Efficiency  
**Needs:** Fast workflow, Search, Drafts

---
